Frequently Asked Questions

What types of clothing do you offer?

We offer all types of clothing, excluding NSFW, graphic content, inappropriate or harmful images/speech and anything that violates Roblox's Terms of Service and Guidelines.

How long will my order take?

Our designers work efficiently to ensure the highest quality of apparel your design will take no longer than 3-7 days.

How do I put in an order?

Join our communications server linked below! All information regarding orders will be there.

What's the pricing structure?

Our pricing depends on the complexity of the clothing/asset. Our minimum pricing for each shirt is r$350.

Can you recreate real-life clothing designs?

Yes, as long as it follows our ordering guidelines.

Can I order clothing assets? e.g: Logos, Awards, etc.

Yes! For more information, speak with a designer via our communications server.
